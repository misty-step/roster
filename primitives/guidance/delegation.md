# Delegation

Commission outcome-shaped lanes with a role, objective, scope, oracle, output
shape, and boundaries. A delegate is another independently resolved Roster
agent: dispatch it with `roster dispatch <name>`. Do not use a Harness-native
child unless the active adapter proves that child's skills, MCPs, model, and
permissions can differ without leaking into the parent. Give critics only the
artifact and oracle, never the author's reasoning trail.
